About the App

VehiWheel is a free educational app designed to help new drivers and learners in New Jersey pass the MVC Knowledge Test. With over 800+ up-to-date questions, interactive practice modes, and access to the official driving manual in multiple formats (PDF, audio, video), VehiWheel provides a complete preparation experience for the 2025 exam.
